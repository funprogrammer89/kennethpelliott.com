Pig [dice game] (Java & C#)

For the uninitiated, Pig is a dice or random numbers game where the objective is to score 100 or more points before your opponent. Each turn you roll as many times as you want adding to your score. If you roll a 1 you loose all points for your turn. You can choose to hold your score at any time adding the points you earned to your total.
